# Influences

## Joel Kowalewski

Research should change how we think about problems, not simply produce results.

Questions are often more enduring than answers.

Scientific rigor and curiosity can coexist with humility.

---

## Robert Atkinson

Human observation remains indispensable.

Technology changes our tools, not our responsibility.

---

## Ryan Wolfe

Better systems produce better information.

Good data emerges from work that people trust.

---

## Kate Kush

Recovery continues long after institutions record success.

Stability is not a destination but an ongoing practice. Language can honor complexity without reducing someone to the hardest chapter of their life.

Writing about people carries responsibility. Stories should create understanding rather than serve as evidence for an argument.
